WELCOME TO NET ZERO!

The Earth is in peril, facing the imminent threat of climate change. As a visionary government, you have been chosen to guide the world towards a sustainable future. Can you rally public support, manage resources wisely, and implement groundbreaking policies to save the planet?

Objective: Achieve net-zero carbon emissions and keep global warming below the critical threshold to secure a thriving future for humanity.

Are you ready to become the savior the world needs?

HOW TO PLAY:

You have three resources you can use to help save the world: funds, public support, and research. With these resources, you can enact policies with the goal of decreasing the global carbon emission rate to net zero.

On the upper left, you will see a date which increments by one month every 2 seconds. Next to it is the global temperature difference above pre-industrial levels.

Keep in mind--if the global temperatures rises to +five degrees above pre-industrial levels, the world ends and you lose the game.

The funds you receive are generated over time, proportional to the percent of public support for your cause (as you collect taxes and revenue).

You also accumulate research "points" constantly over time.

Click on policies to enact them. You must have the necessary resources for them to be be passed. Additionally, once you've enacted a level 1 policy, you might unlock new ones.

The policy colors are as follows--blue policies represent research, yellow policies represent laws to be enacted, and red policies represent initatives. Each will push forth the changes as indicated in the box.

A fast forward button is also available on the upper right-hand.

Good luck, and have fun!
